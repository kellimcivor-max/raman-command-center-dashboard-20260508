The clearest Eaton opportunity is not a single workflow. It is a broader enterprise pattern: important knowledge is spread across systems, work is slowed by manual coordination, and AI value depends on trusted access to the right information at the right time.

  • Reduce dependency on tribal knowledge across frontline and corporate functions
