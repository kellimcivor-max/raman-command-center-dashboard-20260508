Glean for Eaton

Enterprise AI use cases across engineering, operations, service, sales, and corporate functions — designed to help Eaton teams find trusted answers faster, reduce manual work, and scale AI securely across the business.

Why this matters for Eaton

The clearest opportunity is not a single workflow. It is a broader enterprise pattern: important knowledge is spread across systems, work is slowed by manual coordination, and AI value depends on trusted access to the right information at the right time.
